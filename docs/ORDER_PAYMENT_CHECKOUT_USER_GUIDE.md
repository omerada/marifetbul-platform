# ORDER & PAYMENT CHECKOUT - USER GUIDE

## For Buyers (Employers)

### How to Purchase a Service Package

#### Step 1: Find a Service

1. Browse **Marketplace** (`/marketplace`)
2. Use filters to narrow down results:
   - Category
   - Price range
   - Delivery time
   - Seller rating
3. Click on a package to view details

#### Step 2: Review Package Details

- **Description**: What's included
- **Delivery Time**: How long it takes
- **Price**: Total cost + platform fee
- **Seller Info**: Rating, reviews, response time
- **Portfolio**: Previous work samples

#### Step 3: Start Checkout

1. Click **"Satın Al"** (Buy Now) button
2. You'll be redirected to checkout page

#### Step 4: Provide Project Requirements

Fill in the requirements form:

- **Project Details** (required): Describe what you need
- **Ek Notlar** (optional): Additional information
- **Attachments** (if applicable): Upload files

**Tips**:

- Be specific and clear
- Include examples if possible
- Mention deadlines or preferences
- Provide access credentials if needed

#### Step 5: Payment

1. Review order summary:
   - Package price
   - Platform fee (%15)
   - Total amount
   - Delivery date
2. Enter card details:
   - Card number
   - Expiry date (MM/YY)
   - CVC code
   - Postal code
3. Click **"₺XXX Öde"** (Pay)

**Security Note**: Your payment is secured by Stripe. Card details are encrypted and never stored on our servers.

#### Step 6: 3D Secure (if required)

- Your bank may require authentication
- Complete the verification on your bank's page
- You'll be redirected back automatically

#### Step 7: Order Confirmation

After successful payment:

- You'll see the success page
- Confirmation email sent
- Order appears in your dashboard
- Seller gets notified

### Understanding Escrow System

**What is Escrow?**
Escrow (Emanet) protects both buyers and sellers by holding the payment until delivery is confirmed.

**How it Works**:

1. **Payment Made**: Money is held in escrow
2. **Seller Works**: Seller completes the work
3. **Delivery**: Seller delivers the work
4. **Review**: You review and accept/reject
5. **Release**: Money released to seller after acceptance

**Benefits for Buyers**:

- ✅ Payment protected until delivery
- ✅ Can request revisions
- ✅ Refund available if issues
- ✅ No risk of non-delivery

### Managing Your Orders

#### View Orders

Navigate to **Dashboard → Orders** (`/dashboard/employer/orders`)

#### Order Statuses

- **🟡 Beklemede** (Pending): Waiting for seller to start
- **🔵 Devam Ediyor** (In Progress): Seller is working
- **🟣 Teslim Edildi** (Delivered): Work delivered, review needed
- **🟢 Tamamlandı** (Completed): Order complete
- **⚫ İptal Edildi** (Cancelled): Order cancelled
- **🔴 Anlaşmazlık** (Disputed): Issue reported

#### Order Actions

**When Delivered**:

1. **Teslimatı Onayla** (Accept Delivery)
   - Review the work
   - If satisfactory, accept
   - Money released to seller
   - Order completed

2. **Revizyon Talep Et** (Request Revision)
   - If changes needed
   - Explain what needs fixing
   - Seller makes changes
   - Limited revisions (check package)

3. **Anlaşmazlık Bildir** (Report Dispute)
   - If serious issues
   - Admin team reviews
   - Mediation provided
   - Refund may be issued

### Requesting Refunds

#### When Can I Request Refund?

- Seller doesn't deliver on time
- Work doesn't match description
- Serious quality issues
- Seller unresponsive

#### How to Request Refund

1. Go to order detail page
2. Click **"İade Talebi"** button
3. Choose refund type:
   - **Tam İade** (Full Refund): All money back
   - **Kısmi İade** (Partial Refund): Part of money back
4. Enter reason (min 10 characters)
5. Submit request

#### Refund Process

1. **Request Submitted**: Admin notified
2. **Review** (3-5 business days): Case evaluated
3. **Decision**: Approved or rejected
4. **Refund** (7-14 business days): Money returned to card

**Important**:

- Platform fee (%15) is non-refundable
- Provide detailed reason
- Include evidence if available
- Response time: 24-48 hours

## FAQ

### Payment Questions

**Q: Is my card information safe?**
A: Yes, we use Stripe for payment processing. Card details are encrypted and never stored on our servers.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and digital wallets.

**Q: Can I save my card for future use?**
A: Yes, you can securely save cards in your account settings.

**Q: What is the platform fee?**
A: 15% platform fee is added to package price. Shown clearly before payment.

### Escrow Questions

**Q: How long is money held in escrow?**
A: Until buyer accepts delivery, up to 7 days after delivery. Auto-accepts if no action.

**Q: What if I don't accept/reject delivery?**
A: Order auto-accepts after 3 days, money released to seller.

**Q: Can seller access escrow money immediately?**
A: No, money released only after buyer acceptance or auto-accept.

### Refund Questions

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: Approval: 3-5 days, Refund: 7-14 days to card.

**Q: Is platform fee refunded?**
A: No, platform fee is non-refundable.

**Q: Can I get refund after accepting delivery?**
A: Generally no, but contact support for exceptional cases.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Payment failed, what should I do?**
A: Check card details, sufficient funds, and try again. Contact support if persists.

**Q: I didn't receive confirmation email**
A: Check spam folder. View order in dashboard. Contact support if missing.

**Q: 3D Secure not working**
A: Try different browser, disable ad blockers, contact your bank.
