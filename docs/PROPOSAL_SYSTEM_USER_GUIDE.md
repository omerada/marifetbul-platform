## 👨‍💻 For Freelancers

### How to Submit a Proposal

**Step 1: Find a Job**

1. Visit the **Marketplace** from the main menu
2. Browse available jobs or use the search bar
3. Click on a job that interests you to view details

**Step 2: Review Job Requirements**

Before submitting a proposal, make sure you:

- ✅ Meet the required experience level
- ✅ Have the necessary skills
- ✅ Can deliver within the timeline
- ✅ Understand the job requirements

**Step 3: Submit Your Proposal**

1. Click the **"Teklif Ver"** (Submit Proposal) button
2. You'll be redirected to the proposal form
3. Fill in the required information:

   **Cover Letter** (100-2000 characters)
   - Introduce yourself
   - Explain why you're a good fit
   - Highlight relevant experience
   - Address job requirements specifically

   **Proposed Budget**
   - Enter your price in TL
   - Consider the job's budget range
   - Be competitive but fair

   **Delivery Time**
   - Estimate realistic completion time
   - Factor in complexity and workload
   - Consider buffer time for revisions

4. **Optional Fields:**
   - Attach relevant work samples
   - Add milestones if it's a large project
   - Include any questions for the employer

5. Click **"Teklifi Gönder"** (Send Proposal)

**✅ Success!** You'll receive a confirmation and be redirected to your dashboard.

### Managing Your Proposals

#### View All Proposals

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Tekliflerim** (My Proposals)
2. See all your submitted proposals organized by status

#### Proposal Statuses

| Status                          | Meaning                         | Actions Available |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| 🟡 **Bekliyor** (Pending)       | Waiting for employer review     | Withdraw proposal |
| 🔵 **Görüntülendi** (Viewed)    | Employer has seen your proposal | Wait for decision |
| 🟢 **Kabul Edildi** (Accepted)  | Your proposal was accepted!     | Start working     |
| 🔴 **Reddedildi** (Rejected)    | Proposal not selected           | View feedback     |
| ⚫ **Geri Çekildi** (Withdrawn) | You withdrew your proposal      | Cannot undo       |

#### Filtering Proposals

Use the tabs to filter by status:

- **Tümü** (All): View all proposals
- **Bekleyen** (Pending): Active proposals awaiting decision
- **Kabul Edilen** (Accepted): Won proposals
- **Reddedilen** (Rejected): Unsuccessful proposals
- **Geri Çekilen** (Withdrawn): Proposals you withdrew

#### Dashboard Statistics

At the top of your proposals page, you'll see:

- **Total Proposals**: Number of proposals you've submitted
- **Pending**: Proposals waiting for review
- **Accepted**: Proposals that won you work
- **Success Rate**: Percentage of accepted proposals

---

### Withdrawing a Proposal

If you change your mind:

1. Find the proposal in your **Bekleyen** (Pending) tab
2. Click **"Geri Çek"** (Withdraw)
3. Confirm your decision in the dialog

**⚠️ Important:**

- You can only withdraw **pending** proposals
- Withdrawal is **permanent** and cannot be undone
- Accepted proposals cannot be withdrawn (contact employer)
- Frequent withdrawals may affect your reputation

### Tips for Winning Proposals

**✨ Do's:**

- ✅ Customize each proposal for the specific job
- ✅ Show enthusiasm and professionalism
- ✅ Provide relevant examples of your work
- ✅ Be realistic with timeline and budget
- ✅ Proofread before submitting
- ✅ Respond quickly to employer questions

**❌ Don'ts:**

- ❌ Use generic, copy-paste proposals
- ❌ Underbid significantly to win
- ❌ Promise unrealistic delivery times
- ❌ Be vague about your qualifications
- ❌ Submit without reading the job description

## 🏢 For Employers

### Reviewing Proposals

#### View Proposals for Your Jobs

**Option 1: From Dashboard**

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **İş İlanlarım** (My Jobs)
2. You'll see proposal counts on each job card:
   - 🔵 **"5 yeni teklif"** (5 new proposals)
   - 🟡 **"3 bekliyor"** (3 pending)
   - 🟢 **"1 kabul edildi"** (1 accepted)
3. Click **"Teklifleri Gör"** (View Proposals)

**Option 2: From Proposals Page**

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Teklifler** (Proposals)
2. See all proposals across all your jobs
3. Filter by job, status, or date

#### Filter Jobs by Proposal Status

On the jobs page, use filters to find:

- **Tüm İlanlar** (All Jobs)
- **Teklifli İlanlar** (Jobs with Proposals)
- **Yeni Teklifli İlanlar** (Jobs with New Proposals)
- **Bekleyen Teklifli İlanlar** (Jobs with Pending Proposals)
- **Kabul Edilmiş Teklifli İlanlar** (Jobs with Accepted Proposals)

### Viewing Proposal Details

**To view a single proposal:**

1. Click **"Detayları Gör"** (View Details) on any proposal
2. A modal will open showing:

   **Freelancer Information:**
   - Profile photo and name
   - Skills and experience level
   - Success rate and ratings
   - Link to full profile

   **Proposal Details:**
   - Cover letter
   - Proposed budget
   - Estimated delivery time
   - Submitted date
   - Any attachments or work samples

   **Quick Stats:**
   - 💰 Budget comparison with your job's budget
   - ⏱️ Delivery time
   - 📊 Freelancer's proposal success rate

### Comparing Proposals

To compare multiple proposals side-by-side:

1. Select up to 3 proposals (checkboxes)
2. Click **"Karşılaştır"** (Compare)
3. View comparison table showing:
   - Budget differences
   - Delivery times
   - Freelancer ratings
   - Key differences highlighted

**Use comparison to:**

- Quickly identify best value
- Compare qualifications
- Evaluate trade-offs (price vs experience vs speed)

### Accepting or Rejecting Proposals

#### To Accept a Proposal:

1. Review the proposal thoroughly
2. Check freelancer's profile and reviews
3. Click **"Kabul Et"** (Accept)
4. Confirm your decision
5. System will:
   - ✅ Mark proposal as accepted
   - ✅ Notify the freelancer
   - ✅ Create a project/order (if configured)
   - ✅ Update job status

#### To Reject a Proposal:

1. Click **"Reddet"** (Reject)
2. _Optional:_ Provide feedback (helps freelancers improve)
3. Confirm rejection
4. Freelancer will be notified

**💡 Best Practices:**

- Review proposals within 48-72 hours
- Communicate if you need more time
- Provide constructive feedback when rejecting
- Don't ghost freelancers

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### For Freelancers

**Q: Can I submit multiple proposals to the same employer?**  
A: No, you can only submit one proposal per job. Choose wisely!

**Q: Can I edit my proposal after submission?**  
A: Currently, you cannot edit proposals. You can withdraw and resubmit if the job is still open.

**Q: How long should I wait for a response?**  
A: Most employers respond within 3-7 days. Some may take longer or not respond at all.

**Q: Why was my proposal automatically rejected?**  
A: This can happen if:

- The employer closed the job
- The employer accepted another proposal (if configured to auto-reject others)
- The job expired

**Q: What is a good cover letter length?**  
A: Aim for 200-500 characters. Be concise but thorough. Quality over quantity!

**Q: Should I always bid lower to win?**  
A: No! Underbidding can hurt you. Employers value quality and experience. Price competitively but fairly.

**Q: Can I see who else bid on the job?**  
A: No, proposal counts are visible but individual proposals are private.

---

### For Employers

**Q: How many proposals can I accept per job?**  
A: Typically one, but this depends on your job settings. Contact support if you need to hire multiple freelancers.

**Q: Can I negotiate the budget with a freelancer?**  
A: Yes! Use the messaging system to discuss budget and terms before accepting.

**Q: What happens if I don't respond to proposals?**  
A: Proposals remain pending indefinitely, but freelancers may withdraw. It's best practice to respond within a week.

**Q: Can I reject a proposal after accepting it?**  
A: Once accepted, you cannot reject. If you need to cancel, contact the freelancer directly and support if needed.

**Q: How do I know if a freelancer is qualified?**  
A: Check their:

- Profile completion and skills
- Past work reviews and ratings
- Success rate with proposals
- Portfolio and work samples

**Q: Can I contact freelancers before accepting?**  
A: Yes! Use the messaging system to ask questions and clarify details.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### "Teklif Ver butonu görünmüyor" (Submit Proposal button not showing)

**Possible reasons:**

- You've already submitted a proposal to this job
- You're logged in as an employer (not freelancer)
- The job is closed or expired
- Your profile is incomplete

**Solution:**

1. Check your proposal list to see if you already submitted
2. Verify you're logged in with the correct account
3. Check if the job is still open
4. Complete your profile if needed

---

#### "Teklif gönderilirken hata oluştu" (Error submitting proposal)

**Try these steps:**

1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify all required fields are filled correctly
3. Refresh the page and try again
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser
6. Contact support if issue persists

---

#### "Tekliflerim yüklenmiyor" (My proposals not loading)

**Solutions:**

1. Refresh the page (F5)
2. Check if you're logged in
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try incognito/private mode
5. Check system status page
6. Report if problem continues

---

#### "Bildirimler gelmiyor" (Not receiving notifications)

**Check:**

1. Notification settings are enabled
2. Browser notifications are allowed
3. You're logged in
4. No browser extensions blocking notifications
5. Refresh the page to trigger polling

---

#### "Teklifimi geri çekemiyorum" (Can't withdraw proposal)

**Reasons:**

- Proposal has already been accepted/rejected
- Proposal status has changed
- Network connectivity issue

**Solution:**

1. Refresh the page to see current status
2. If proposal is accepted, contact employer directly
3. Contact support if you believe there's an error
